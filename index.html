Troubleshooting

PocketSnap+ Won’t Open

  • Force quit + reopen
  • Check internet connection
  • Restart device
  • Reinstall app (synced data is safe)

Login Issues

  • Use Forgot Password
  • Check email spelling
  • Look in spam/junk

Slow Performance

  • Free up device storage
  • Close background apps
  • Reinstall if needed
