Getting Started

Download & Install

  • Get PocketSnap+ from the App Store
  • Requires iOS 18.6 or later
  • Open and start snapping!

Troubleshooting

PocketSnap+ Won’t Open

  • Force quit + reopen
  • Restart device
  • Reinstall app (synced data is safe)

Slow Performance

  • Free up device storage
  • Close background apps
  • Reinstall if needed
